# Bounty Hunter Reel Campaign Images

Ten vertical marketing stills for Instagram Reels, Shorts, carousels, and pitch visuals.
They are intentionally emotional and cinematic rather than explanatory.

## Assets

1. `01-minimal-ledger-of-effort.png` - Elegant hero still: invisible effort becoming visible value.
2. `02-bold-guild-heist-energy.png` - Bold guild/crew energy: missions as playful high-stakes coordination.
3. `03-funny-proof-kitchen-victory.png` - Comedic proof moment: a tiny chore treated like a heroic achievement.
4. `04-premium-app-product-shot.png` - Product-style phone mock: the app as a desirable object.
5. `05-aspirational-loot-vault.png` - Reward Store emotion: credits opening a private vault of rewards.
6. `06-family-mode-earned-pride.png` - Family Mode: chores turning into pride and recognition.
7. `07-couple-mode-private-gift.png` - Couple Mode: favors, tokens, and gifts as a private ritual.
8. `08-storyboard-grid-core-loop.png` - Storyboard/grid: mission, proof, approval, credits, reward.
9. `09-proof-trophy-wall.png` - History/trophy wall: completed efforts becoming remembered.
10. `10-surreal-reward-universe-key-art.png` - Reel cover/key art: everyday tasks orbiting the reward economy.

## Direction

Core visual language: dark indigo/black, cyan holographic UI, amber credit glow, restrained red helmet accent.

Use the cleaner lifestyle images for broad audience ads, the guild images for the original sci-fi identity,
and the storyboard/grid image when you need to communicate the loop quickly without turning it into a tutorial.
