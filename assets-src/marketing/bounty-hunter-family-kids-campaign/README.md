# Bounty Hunter Family Kids Campaign

Minimal Family Mode campaign assets for a parent-and-child room-cleaning story.
The set follows the calmer product/lifestyle direction from the minimal campaign:
warm home lighting, matte graphite surfaces, restrained cyan UI, muted amber tokens,
and subtle red logo cues.

## Storyboard

1. `01-panel-mom-creates-room-mission.png` - Mother creates a room-cleaning mission.
2. `02-panel-child-cleans-room.png` - Child cleans and organizes the room.
3. `03-panel-child-proof-photo.png` - Child captures proof of the tidy room.
4. `04-panel-mom-approves-proof.png` - Mother approves the proof.
5. `05-panel-token-reward-moment.png` - Mother gives the child an earned token.
6. `06-panel-earned-reward-shelf.png` - Child sees the grounded reward object.

## Direction

This is for Family Mode rather than Couple Mode. It shows a kid earning recognition
for cleaning their room, while keeping the tone premium and emotionally grounded
instead of overly magical or AI-stylized.
