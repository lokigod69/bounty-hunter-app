# Bounty Hunter Minimal Campaign

Refined vertical campaign assets with a calmer, Apple-like product/lifestyle aesthetic.
This set keeps the Bounty Hunter identity through matte graphite surfaces, restrained cyan UI,
muted amber credits, and a subtle red helmet-inspired accent, without the heavier particle/glow style.

## Storyboard

1. `01-panel-invitation-mission-card.png` - A mission arrives quietly on the phone.
2. `02-panel-quiet-task-start.png` - The task begins as a small private ritual.
3. `03-panel-proof-capture.png` - Proof is captured with low-friction calm.
4. `04-panel-quiet-approval.png` - Approval becomes a small moment of recognition.
5. `05-panel-earned-credit-token.png` - A single physical credit makes effort tangible.
6. `06-panel-reward-object.png` - The reward feels desirable, practical, and earned.

## Direction

Use this set when the previous campaign feels too AI-stylized or too spectacle-heavy.
The tone here is premium, tactile, quiet, and social: missions and rewards as a private,
beautiful system for making effort visible.
